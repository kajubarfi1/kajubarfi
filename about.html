01 — About

howdy! i'm lehana.

I'm a senior Electrical Engineering student at Texas A&M University with minors in Mathematics and Technical Sales, driven by the intersection of technology and the customer. In the semiconductor and chip industry, I want to work where technical depth meets real-world impact and help customers get the most out of the technology they're building with.

On campus, I plan engineering events for the Society of Women Engineers, and drum with TAMU Percussion Studio. Outside of school, I love watching anime and movies, making and listening to music, and grabbing iced coffee with my friends.

about kaju coffeehouse

Anyone who knows me knows my undying love for coffee (and my overambitious dream of opening a coffee shop/flower shop/bookstore/bakery/planetarium). Kaju is the hindi word for cashew, and, in the context of this website, is short for my favorite indian sweet, kaju barfi.
